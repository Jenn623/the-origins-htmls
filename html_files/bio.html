DPR IAN: MITO

"MITO" is a musical proyect by Artist Cristian Yu, known as DPR IAN, launched in 2020

MITO is an "Alter Ego" that represents IAN'S internal struggles and personal demons from IAN, the other side from his creative and extrovert self

This proyect is composed by the following universes:

  • Mood Swings In This Order
  • Mood Swings In To Order
  • Dear Insanity...
  • Saint

Song you need to hear to understand MITO'S universe (listen in order):

  1. So Beutiful
  2. Nerves
  3. No Blueberries
  4. Seraph
  5. Peanut Butter & Tears
  6. Don't go Insane
  7. Saint
